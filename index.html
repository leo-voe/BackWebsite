How to bake Butter-Cookies
Step 1: Mix dry ingredients

Through a sieve add 280g flour, 1 tsp of baking powder, 26g of vanilla sugar, 100g starch and 160g sugar to a bowl and mix it shortly.

Step 2: Add egg yolks

Add 4 egg yolks to the mixture

Step 2: Add cubed butter

Slice 240g of cold butter into small cubes add to mixture.
