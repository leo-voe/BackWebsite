How to bake Butter-Cookies
Step 1: Mix dry ingredients

Through a sieve add 280g flour, 1 tsp of baking powder, 26g of vanilla sugar, 100g starch and 160g sugar to a bowl and mix it shortly.

Step 2: Add egg yolks

Add 4 egg yolks to the mixture.

Step 3: Add cubed butter

Slice 240g of cold butter into small cubes add to mixture.

Step 4: Knead into ball

Mix the ingredience and knead the mixture until formed into a ball like on the image.

Step 5: Let dough rest

Let the dough rest for atleast 1 hour in the fridge wrapped in cling film.

Step 6: Roll dough out

Take a reasonable portion of your dough and roll it out with a rolling pin. Put the rest of the dough back into the fridge for now.

Step 7: Use cookie cutters

Use your cookie cutters and put the cutted shapes onto a baking tray with baking paper.

Step 8: Mix icing

Use egg yolks and a little water to create a icing which can be applied to the cookies before baking.

Step 9: Bake cookies

Bake cookies at 200 degree celsius circulating air for about 6 minutes until golden.

Step 10: Let cookies cool

Put cookies on a cooling tray of sort, and let them cool.
